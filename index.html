Pug

Pugs are one of three types of short-nosed dogs that are known to have been bred by the Chinese: the Lion dog, the Pekingese, and the Lo-sze, which was the ancient Pug.

  • Scientific name: Canis lupus familiaris
  • Average length:10-14 inches
  • Average lifespan: 12-15 years
  • Physical featutes: Short,smooth

The Pug is a breed of dog with physically distinctive features of a wrinkly, short-muzzled face, and curled tail.The Pug,also called the Chinese Pug, Dutch Mastiff and Dutch Bulldog.Pugs are lively, alert animals that behave lovingly toward people of all ages and get along well with other dogs.
